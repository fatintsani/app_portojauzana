Art Division Team – Student Cultural Organization (UKM Daerah)

In this organization, I actively participated as a member of the Art Division Team in a regional student organization that frequently organizes cultural events and concerts on campus.

In my role, I was responsible for designing and preparing the stage concept and event decoration, ensuring that each event had a visually appealing and thematic atmosphere. This included planning stage layouts, selecting decorative elements, and coordinating the visual concept to match the theme of the event or performance.

I also worked collaboratively with other divisions to ensure the stage setup was aligned with technical requirements such as lighting, performance flow, and audience experience. During event preparation, I was involved in both the planning phase (concept design) and execution phase (on-site setup and arrangement).

Through this experience, I developed strong skills in teamwork, creativity, time management, and event execution, especially in handling large-scale events such as concerts and cultural performances.
